With an ‘Ambition to Create Novel Expressions’, Acne Studios was founded in Stockholm in 1996. Founder Jonny Johansson takes a multidisciplinary approach to fashion, combining his interests in art, architecture and design to create a brand that incorporates ready-to-wear, furniture, exhibitions and publishing. Shape and proportion, attention to detail and an eclectic mix of fabrics give Acne Studios a distinct style. Runway pieces and modern tailoring sit alongside the signature Face collection and the renowned denim line.
